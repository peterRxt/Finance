📚 Hardware Store Ledgers Guide (Kenya)

🧾 1. Financial Ledgers

Ledger NamePurpose
Cash LedgerTrack all cash receipts and payments
Bank LedgerTrack bank deposits, withdrawals, and charges
Sales Ledger (Debtors)Track credit sales and customer balances
Purchase Ledger (Creditors)Track credit purchases and supplier balances
General LedgerSummarize all accounts and postings
Journal LedgerNon-cash adjustments and corrections
Petty Cash LedgerTrack minor daily cash expenses

📦 2. Inventory Ledgers

Ledger NamePurpose
Stock/Inward LedgerRecord goods received from suppliers
Stock/Outward LedgerRecord goods sold or used
Stock Balance LedgerTrack item-wise quantity and value
Damaged/Returns LedgerRecord damaged or returned goods

👨‍💼 3. Customer & Supplier Ledgers

Ledger NamePurpose
Customer LedgerTrack sales and payments from each customer
Supplier LedgerTrack purchases and payments to each supplier
Credit Sales RegisterSummary of credit sales transactions
Credit Purchases RegisterSummary of credit purchases

💳 4. Expense & Income Ledgers

Ledger NamePurpose
Rent & Utilities LedgerTrack regular overheads like rent and electricity
Salaries & Wages LedgerTrack employee payments, NHIF, NSSF, PAYE
Marketing LedgerTrack advertising, branding, promotion costs
Repairs LedgerTrack repairs and equipment maintenance
Miscellaneous Income LedgerRecord small non-core incomes (e.g. interest)

🏛️ 5. Statutory Ledgers (Kenya)

Ledger NamePurpose
VAT LedgerTrack VAT on purchases and sales
NHIF/NSSF LedgerTrack statutory contributions
PAYE LedgerRecord employee income tax deductions
Withholding Tax LedgerRecord withholding taxes on services

🧮 Optional Advanced Ledgers

Ledger NamePurpose
Loan LedgerTrack loan amounts, interest, repayments
Fixed Asset LedgerTrack assets, value, depreciation
Mobile Money LedgerTrack M-PESA transactions
Discount LedgerTrack discounts given or received
Customer Deposits LedgerRecord advance or lay-by deposits
