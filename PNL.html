üìä Structure of a P&L Statement

1. Revenue (Sales)

Total income from product or service sales.

Examples: cement, paint, tools, rentals.

Formula: Total Revenue = Sum of all sales

2. Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

Direct cost of acquiring goods sold.

Examples: inventory purchases, delivery fees.

Formula: COGS = Opening Inventory + Purchases - Closing Inventory
Or simplified: COGS ‚âà Total Purchases

3. Gross Profit

Formula: Gross Profit = Revenue - COGS

4. Operating Expenses

Day-to-day running costs.

  • Salaries & Wages
  • Rent & Utilities
  • Marketing & Advertising
  • Repairs, Internet, Supplies

5. Other Income/Expenses

Non-core business activities like bank interest or fines.

6. Net Profit (or Loss)

Formula: Net Profit = Gross Profit - Operating Expenses + Other Income - Other Expenses
