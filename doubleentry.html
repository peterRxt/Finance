📘 Ledger Debit & Credit Guide

🔁 Double Entry Rule

Every transaction affects two ledgers: one is Debited, one is Credited.

Debits must always equal Credits.

🧮 Debit and Credit Cheat Sheet

TypeDebit When...Credit When...
AssetsIncreaseDecrease

EquityDecreaseIncrease
Income (Sales)DecreaseIncrease
ExpensesIncreaseDecrease

🧠 Summary Rules

  • Asset Increase → Debit
  • Expense Increase → Debit
  • Liability Increase → Credit
  • Income Increase → Credit
